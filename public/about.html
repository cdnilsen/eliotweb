Eliotweb is a site-in-progress for browsing the text of the Massachusett-language Eliot Bible, along with Experience Mayhew's 1709 translation of the Psalms and Gospel of John; eventually, I also intend to add the texts of various secondary missionary texts and sermons. It is still a work in progress. Many Old Testament books are still awaiting copies, and very little proofreading of any section of the text has been done so far. Nothing should be cited without consulting scans of original copies.

There are a number of people to whom this site owes a considerable debt: Ives Goddard and David Costa for their considerable body of knowledge and willingness to answer questions; Wesley Rian and Cazim Hysi for their programming expertise; and Drew Bennett and James Bertrand for their ongoing feedback on the site's structure.
