Eliotweb is a site-in-progress for browsing the text of the Massachusett-language Eliot Bible, along with Experience Mayhew's 1709 translation of the Psalms and Gospel of John; eventually, I also intend to add the texts of various secondary missionary texts and sermons. It is still a work in progress. Many Old Testament books are still awaiting copies, and very little proofreading has been done. Nothing should be cited without consulting scans of original copies.

I am indebted to a number of people for their help and guidance: Ives Goddard and David Costa for their considerable body of knowledge and willingness to answer questions; Wesley Rian and Cazim Hysi for their programming expertise; and Drew Bennett and James Bertrand for their ongoing feedback on the site's structure.
